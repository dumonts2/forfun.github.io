Roblox

What Is Roblox?

Roblox is an online game platform and game creation system that was released in 2006 by David Baszucki and Erik Cassell. This platform not only allowed people to play an unlimited amount of games, but it also allowed people to create an unlimited amount of games or maps.

People who play Roblox get to decorate their own block looking avatar. Roblox contains a large market for accesories that can be bought using Robux (which is bought with real money). The most popular games on Roblox at the time when I played it most were: fighting games either with swords or guns, paintball courses, obstacle courses, tycoons, roleplays, and survival games.

My Experience Playing

I personally loved playing Roblox when I was younger and all of my siblings did as well. My older step-brother would even design his own games at times. My favorite games on roblox were Obbys (short for obstacle courses),Survive the Disasters, Warrior Cats roleplay, and fashion shows.
